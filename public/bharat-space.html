Gaganyaan Programme

Objective: Demonstrate human spaceflight capability by launching a crew of 3 members to an orbit of 400 km for a 3-day mission and bringing them back safely to earth.

Astronaut Training: Astronaut designates (Vyomanauts) undergo rigorous physical, psychological, and technical training, initially in Russia and now at ISRO facilities.

Launch Vehicle: The LVM3 rocket, reconfigured to meet human rating requirements, will be used as the launch vehicle.

Timeline: Uncrewed tests are ongoing, with the crewed mission targeted for the near future.
